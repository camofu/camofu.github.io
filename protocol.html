Current work

WHIR — independent research and contributions

October 2025 – present · Remote

Ongoing research and open-source contributions to WHIR — a multilinear polynomial commitment scheme based on an IOP of proximity for constrained Reed–Solomon codes — and its reference Rust implementation whir-p3 (originally tcoratger/whir-p3, now upstreaming into Plonky3/Plonky3). The first three months (October – December 2025) were paid contract work for the Ethereum Foundation, delivered through Lita. After Lita closed I continued voluntarily, including protocol design (batch opening, HVZK overlay) and a pending WHIR-DAS grant proposal.
