About

I'm a cryptographer working at the intersection of zero-knowledge proof systems and distributed systems. My recent work spans WHIR and multilinear polynomial commitment schemes, zkVM architecture (Valida), and privacy-preserving protocols (shielded airdrop, Namada specs). Before cryptography I worked on quantum machine learning and semiconductor-based qubit hardware.
